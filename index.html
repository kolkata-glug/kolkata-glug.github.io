Kolkata GNU/Linux User Group


What is it?

User Groups originated in 1970s as physical places where users of a certain technology met to share their knowledge. With the creation of the GNU/Linux operating system, GLUGs started flourishing in the 90s and later with the spread of internet, many user groups grew up on the internet. More info on GLUGs here.

Example of a GNU/Linux User Group. Located in Beijing, China.

We are Kolkata GLUG, an user group started in 2022 and based in Kolkata, India. However people from all over the world are welcome to join us. We started online but hope to organize physical meetups in the near future, essentially we follow "hybrid model".

Join us!

If you are interested to join us, all you have to do is join our matrix room by clicking HERE.

We also have an IRC channel and a XMPP room but have been abandoned.

Follow the GLUG's social media pages: X (Twitter), Fediverse, GitHub.

Our Vision

  • Advocating computing freedom through promotion of Swatantra Software (is called Free/Libre software in english)
  • Reviving Hacker culture (It's not the same as cybercrime or cybersecurity, read here)
  • Advocating online privacy, security and net neutrality
